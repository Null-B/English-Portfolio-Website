# Protfolio 9 My letter to youMy letter to you

Dear Mr Heeg,

Thank you for the last two years of some of the best English classes I have ever had. It was a blast having you as my teacher, and I will surely never forget you.

I will miss the chaos in our classes and all the funny moments we shared throughout the school year. English wasn't just about learning the language—it was also about learning as a person. You showed us that it's possible to learn while having fun.

What will stay with me the most is your sense of humor and your way of teaching. I truly believe that the best way to learn a language is by actually using it, and you did an amazing job of making people enjoy speaking English instead of seeing it as a chore. I think that's something you can definitely take with you to PHZH.

Another thing I learned from your class is that we're all just people. Even teachers with 30 years of experience are still human, and I really appreciated that you never pretended otherwise.

Sadly, we didn't get a "baddie" teacher in English—but you did the job.

I wish you all the best for the future, and I hope you have as much fun with your future classes as you had with ours.

Best wishes,

Kemal
