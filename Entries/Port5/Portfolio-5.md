# Protfolio 5 Best of 2025
1. Have you heard any albums from Pitchfork’s Best Albums of 2025 list? What did you think?
Yes. I recognized a few artists from the list, like Bad Bunny, Sudan Archives, FKA twigs, and MIKE. I wasn’t familiar with most of the albums before, but the ones I listened to had different styles — some were cool, some were weird. I liked the ones that had stronger beats and rhythms. Pitchfork

2. Which 2025 albums from the list did you listen to, and which one did you like the most? Why?
I listened to bits from:
    • Bad Bunny — DeBÍ TiRAR MáS FOToS (lots of rhythm and energy),
    • Sudan Archives — The BPM (interesting mix of violin and beat),
    • FKA twigs — EUSEXUA (weird but had some good parts),
    • MIKE — Showbiz! (rap vibes I liked),
    • KP Skywalka — I Tried to Tell You (fun to hear new rap). Pitchfork
Favorite: **MIKE — Showbiz! ** because it was closest to the kind of rap I normally enjoy and had more relatable flow and lyrics for me. Pitchfork

3. Are there any 2025 albums NOT on this list that you think should be included? Which ones, and why?
I think some hip-hop albums that were big in the community but didn’t make the Pitchfork list should be included — ones that other fans liked a lot. For example, some rap albums that charted or had popular tracks in 2025 should count too, even if critics didn’t pick them.
